Building software & the people who build it

I'm a passionate technology leader, architect, and mentor based in the Madison, Wisconsin area. As a Software Development Director at Trek Bicycle, I lead teams that design and ship the platforms behind one of the world's most recognized brands.

My story starts long before the job titles — I wrote my first code at 12 years old and never stopped. Computers and games have been the constant thread through my entire life, and that early obsession grew into a deep love for clean architecture, cloud technology, and solving hard problems at scale.

I care just as much about people as I do about systems. I believe in leading by example — never asking my team to do something I wouldn't do myself — and I genuinely love helping others learn and grow. My philosophy is simple: there's a solution to every problem, and winners find a way.
